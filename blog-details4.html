Poem

Words

  • By Paprja Apurvam
  • November 19, 2019
  • No Comments

Our lives are one big puzzle, We don’t know how many pieces we have got,
There are people who are trying to fit in, And people who try but do not.

Everyone has their own puzzle , There will be the ones in which don’t fit,
Don’t you dare to make your piece smaller, Just so u can live there for a bit.

Some day we laughed until we cried, Someday we cried until we laughed,
Either way , Everyday, Was perfect!!!

Donot regret what u missed And donot miss what you regret.

Sometimes there is no support system, Its just gonna be you and your grind.
Sometimes you are all you will have, And sometimes that’s all you will ever need!

"Whats the point of being alive if you don’t at least try to do something remarkable?"

John Green
American Author

So just be at peace but not satisfied With all that had happened,
Is happening And will happen!!!

One day everything will be perfect, they way you wanted it to be. So for now,just laugh at the confusions,smile through the tears
And remind yourself that everything happens for a reason. Always remember one thing,
The best view comes after the hardest climb!
